# memnon-archive
Archival materials dating back to 1980's. Research/musings and related collaborative artistic and technical work. The collection includes publications, notebook entries, engineering sketches, photographs, videos, spreadsheets, experimental data, historical references, and unfinished ideas. The materials vary considerably in age, completeness, and organization. Some are preserved primarily as source material—or “compost”—for future research and design.

Related repositories

Memnon Challenge — the public introduction and invitation to explore or extend the work

Memnon Futures — new concepts, AI-assisted research, compositional ideas, and speculative future developments

The memnon-archive is a work in progress. Materials may be reorganized and described more fully as the collection develops.
